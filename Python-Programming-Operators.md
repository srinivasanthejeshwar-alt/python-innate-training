# 🐍 Python Programming - Operators

## 📚 Topic: Python Operators

This program demonstrates the different types of operators available in Python.

### Concepts Covered

- Arithmetic Operators
- Assignment Operators
- Comparison Operators
- Logical Operators
- Formatted Output (f-Strings)

---

## 💻 Full Program

```python
# Arithmetic and Assignment Operators

a = 10
b = 20
c = a + b
print(f"The addition of two numbers is {c}")

a1 = 50
b1 = 60
a1 -= b1
print(f"The subtraction of two numbers is\n{a1}")

a2 = 50
b2 = 50
a2 *= b2
print("The multiplication of the two numbers is", a2)

a4 = 81
b4 = 56
c2 = a4 / b4
print(f"The division of the two numbers is\n{c2:.3f}")

a5 = 9
b5 = 6
a5 %= b5
print("The remainder of the two numbers is", a5)

# Comparison Operators

d = 56
e = 97

print(f"The comparison of the two numbers is {d == e}")
print(f"The comparison of the two numbers is {d != e}")
print(f"The comparison of the two numbers is {d > e}")
print(f"The comparison of the two numbers is {d < e}")
print(f"The comparison of the two numbers is {d >= e}")
print(f"The comparison of the two numbers is {d <= e}")

# Logical Operators

f = 89
g = 96

print(f"The logic of the two numbers is {f > 0 and g < 100}")
print(f"The logic of the two numbers is {f > 0 or g < 100}")
print(f"The logic of the two numbers is {not(f > 0 and g < 100)}")
```
